Gets or sets, whether metadata information should be retrieved.

Only supported by GetCollectionMoviesAsync(TraktExtendedOption), GetCollectionShowsAsync(TraktExtendedOption), GetCollectionMoviesAsync(String, TraktExtendedOption) and GetCollectionShowsAsync(String, TraktExtendedOption). Will be ignored otherwise.

Only supported by GetWatchedShowsAsync(TraktExtendedOption) and GetWatchedShowsAsync(String, TraktExtendedOption). Will be ignored otherwise.
